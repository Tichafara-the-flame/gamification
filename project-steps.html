Project Details

TM1 KICK-OFF MEETING IN POLANDL

The TM1 – Kick-Off Meeting took place in Łódź, Poland. The first meeting was hosted by Europejska Fundacja Rozwoju Człowieka, the applicant of the project. Coordinators and Experts met from 3rd to 8th July 2021 to launch the project and manage internal aspects of it after 10 months of cooperation online (due to the pandemic).
Recent Details

TM2 PARTNERS’ UPDATE IN MACEDONIA

The TM2 Partners’ Update took place in Ohrid and Skopje, Macedonia. The second meeting was hosted by the Macedonian partner KRIK. Coordinators and Experts met from 21st till 25th of September 2021 to evaluate and assess the status of the IO1 (and its games) plus considering the next steps for the partnership.
Project Details

TM3 PARTNERS’ UPDATE IN ESTONIA

The TM3 Partners’ Update took place in Tartu, Estonia. The third meeting was hosted by the Estonian partner TNTK. Coordinators and Experts met from 21st to 25th November to prepare for the finalization of the IO1 and arrange the last 9 months of the project and its LTTA basis.
