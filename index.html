PROJECT STEPS

The project was divided into parts

TM1 KICK-OFF MEETING IN POLAND

The TM1 – Kick-Off Meeting took place in Łódź, Poland. The first meeting was hosted by Europejska Fundacja Rozwoju Człowieka, the applicant of the project.

TM2 PARTNERS’ UPDATE IN MACEDONIA

The TM2 Partners’ Update took place in Ohrid and Skopje, Macedonia. The second meeting was hosted by the Macedonian partner KRIK.

TM3 PARTNERS’ UPDATE IN ESTONIA

The TM3 Partners’ Update took place in Tartu, Estonia. The third meeting was hosted by the Estonian partner TNTK.
